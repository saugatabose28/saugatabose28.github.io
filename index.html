Saugata Bose

With a recently submitted PhD in Computer Science, I specialize in enhancing Natural Language Processing (NLP) systems using cutting-edge techniques, such as BERT and One-Class classification. My extensive skill set spans supervised learning, feature extraction, and algorithm optimization, and I'm proficient in Python, Scikit-Learn, NumPy, PyTorch, and TensorFlow. My expertise is underlined by four published research papers and certifications in deep learning and business modeling. I have a track record of practical application, from improving software quality and agile IT transformation to developing machine learning models for classification, clustering, and anomaly detection. My dedication to innovation is evident in my pioneering work on hate speech detection models. I am actively seeking opportunities to apply my expertise in ML and NLP to drive innovation in the automation industry.
